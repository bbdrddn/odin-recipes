Peach & Mango Protein Ice Cream

There's two things fatties and people who workout can agree upon. No, one of them isn't that being obese is a healthy thing, sit down. Anyways, those two things are 1. So one day, some of those guy with more braincells than the average person come together and engineered a recipe so close to real ice cream it's not uncanny, it becomes a dish of its own, and it satisfies both our sweet tooth and protein macros while keeping the calories low. The challenge of this recipe is that you need to invest in two things: protein powder and a portable blender (either a Ninja or Magic Bullet). And this recipe also introduce an ingredient some thought to be engineered chemical but is actually natural. Xanthan Gum, which is what makes our smoothie bind like fat molecules in ice cream. Other than that, this recipe is as easy as making scrambled eggs (You do know how to make it right ?)

Ingredients

  • 150g Frozen Mango
  • 150g Frozen Peaches
  • 1 scoop Protein Powder
  • 120ml Milk of choice (or use water if you're a brokie)
  • 1/4 tbsp Xanthan Gum
  • 1/2 tbsp Honey or Stevia (any kind)
  • Very little Salt (to amplify sweetness)

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in your blender

    For a portable blender, do this in two seperate batches, for a classic family style blender, go all out and serve in portions

  2. Blend all ingredients together

    It is up to your choice to blend it in pulses or one single run. But i recommend going in long pulses, then open to check or scrape anything that sticks to the side of your blender so it blends and incorporate evenly. A proper blend with the help of Xanthan Gum will have a consistency 85-90% similar to real ice cream while having little to no fat content.

  3. Serve in a bowl or eat from the blender cup as is

    Since we're covering the two cases where you either using a portable blender or a home blender, please serve it in a bowl and share it with friends and family when you use a big blender. Please understand that as delicious, sweet and low calories as this protein ice cream is, treats and desserts taste best when you share it with your loved ones, savor the flavor and the emotional experience you and people close to you share.
